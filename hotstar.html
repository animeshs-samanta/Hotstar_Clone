Mission Mongal

Mission Mangal tells the story of the women and men who took India to Mars. Vidya Balan is wonderful as Tara Shinde, a scientist who must juggle her research with wifely and motherly duties. She gives the narrative a boisterous can-do spirit that is eventually well matched by Akshay Kumar's Dhawan.
